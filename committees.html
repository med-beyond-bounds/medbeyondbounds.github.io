Committee Roles

We're a 501(c)(3) recognized student led nonprofit focused on improving access to healthcare through free over the counter essential stock stations, care packages, volunteering, and health education. This form will help place you into one of our three core committees. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, and we're excited to have you on board.

Social Media and Marketing Committee run by Megha George: Help us spread our mission by managing social media, designing graphics, collaborating with other organizations, and finding new ways to build community. This is the perfect role for creatives, communicators, and content creators.

Health Education and Advocacy Committee run by Mahee Mishra: This team will produce a podcast series, Whispers of the White Coat, to highlight patient stories, promote health literacy, and give a voice to those in need. You'll help us research topics, interview guests, and design educational content.

Fundraising Committee run by Tanya Kari: Raise funds for our projects through events, bake sales, merch, and grants. This team is ideal for those interested in budgeting, marketing, and strategic planning to keep our mission running strong.
