Why should you join MBB?

Med Beyond Bounds gives students the opportunity to make a direct impact on healthcare access in underserved communities through hands on service, outreach, and advocacy. Our organization runs through a committee based structure, meaning every member actively contributes to real projects from the moment they join. Each member is automatically placed into a committee, where they gain practical experience in areas such as outreach, health education, and fundraising.

Because committees drive our work, every member takes on leadership responsibilities and develops skills in collaboration, organization, and community engagement. This structure also creates a clear pathway for members to grow into officer positions and help shape the future of MBB.

To join us, fill out our committee form. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, so you can apply at any time. If you have any further questions, feel free to reach out to us via our contacts below. We look forward to hearing from you.
