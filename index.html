About Us

Med Beyond Bounds is a 501(c)(3) recognized student-led non-profit organization based in Bryan-College Station dedicated to advancing healthcare equity in underserved areas throughout Texas. With the distribution of over the counter medical essentials via stock stations, care packages, health education, and advocacy resources, we hope to improve access to healthcare. MBB's mission is simple: break barriers in healthcare and bring comfort and resources to those who need it the most.
